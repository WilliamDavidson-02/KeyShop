Our values

Loving the mechanical keyboard is equivalent to replacing the lonely moments in life with moments of great enjoyment. A person does not have to be rich and noble, and a small wealth is also an attitude to life. But it's a big pity that you can't sleep with your beloved mechanical keyboard pillow in your dreams! KBDfans attaches great importance to the needs of keyboard enthusiasts, weakens the so-called operation, and puts more energy into original design to catch up in dreams and fantasies.
